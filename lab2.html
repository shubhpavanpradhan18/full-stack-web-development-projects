About

Hackathon 2026 is a national-level innovation event designed to encourage students to develop creative solutions for real-world problems using modern technologies. Participants will collaborate in teams, build innovative prototypes, and present their ideas before industry experts and mentors. The event promotes learning, teamwork, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking.
